Who We Are

Our Company

Established in 1998 as the pioneering Rent a Car Company by the managing director Miss. M. A. Dilsha Mallawaarachchie along with her amazing team Mr. D. M. G. Bimsara Nawarathna, Mr. Imran Rasheed, Mr. S. A. Wijesinghe & Miss. T. Roshain Fernando. BIRDSJET (Pvt) Ltd. is a top managed business that reign at the top of rental cars Sri Lanka companies, offering the widest range of transportation services using from a choice of small budget cars to luxury limousines and security vehicles and many other options. Our services extend to corporate group events, VIP movements, weddings & celebrity events and any other requirements. With the largest ever vehicle fleet to date since its inception. BIRDSJET has always maintained consistency and excelled in providing the highest levels of customer service quality and satisfaction with highly personalised service to all our customers.

We have always lived up to our company motto, “Excellence Unmatched” beyond mere words with the secret of success of our company being a team of dedicated and dynamic staff members striving to go the extra mile in improving the quality of service offered to each and every customer since the company’s inception.

BIRDSJET continues to maintain its image and reputation as the specialists in this business in Sri Lanka besides being the leading budget company in the island as well.

Our Vision

To lead our industry by defining service excellence and building unmatched customer loyalty.


Our Strength

With a dynamic and dedicated team of 5 Marketing & Operational staff, to serve our customers speedily & efficiently with fluency in speaking country’s main three languages, we strongly believe that our loyal staff members are our key strength in carrying out day to day operations staying attuned to the ever-changing business dynamics, challenges and service environment.

Our Clients

Our loyal clientele consists of the largest and the most diverse segments comprising individuals, companies and groups who make use of our vehicle rental services for all types of purposes. Our customer base includes individual customers both local & foreign visiting Sri Lanka for business & leisure as well as government & nongovernmental organisations, corporate and diplomatic missions. We have earned many commendations as well as received endorsements & compliments from both organisations and individual customers for our relentless, untiring effort as a company to better the services offered.
